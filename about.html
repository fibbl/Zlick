Shape your company’s future with Projectile

Project management software shouldn’t make life harder, it should be a strategic partner in leading your team and enable you to do your best work. That’s why we built Projectile.

Slide 7 of 10.
Trusted by INDUSTRY leaders

Projectile enables your team to work more efficiently from a distance.

Coming from an extensive background in building and selling SaaS platforms, our founders realized that the tool that helps you get your work done is one of the most important ones.

Despite that, the market lacked a powerful project management tool that is genuinely easy to use, and purpose-built for modern hybrid work setups. That’s what led to the birth of Projectile.
